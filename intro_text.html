WELCOME TO TimeScout

Your essential tool for accessible time series analysis and forecasting.

TimeScout is an intuitive, user-friendly R Shiny application designed to simplify time series modeling. This tool supports fetching and processing diverse economic data from FRED, making it perfect for users ranging from beginners in economic forecasting to seasoned analysts seeking a streamlined solution.

WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH TimeScout:

  • Access and visualize economic data instantly.
  • Choose between raw, de-seasonalized, or de-trended data processing options.
  • Model and forecast using ARIMA and SARIMA models.
  • Evaluate model performance with comprehensive metrics like RMSFE and AIC.
  • Compare results conveniently in your analysis.

IMPORTANT: Default page is an example dashboard.
